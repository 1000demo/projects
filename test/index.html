Profile

Dr. Manoj Namdeo Gulhane is a Consultant Neurologist based in Nashik, with over two decades of experience in the medical profession. He practises currently at the Brain N Nerves Clinic (Canada Corner) and the Suyash Hospital (Mumbai Naka). Dr. Gulhane has much experience and expertise in the treatment of epilepsy, headache and stroke. He also specialises in multiple sclerosis, neuropathy and muscle/spine problems.

Dr. Gulhane completed his post-graduation (MD - Medicine) from GMC Nagpur in 1990. With neurology as his choice of super-specialisation, he then obtained the postdoctoral DM (Neuro) superspeciality degree from Mumbai University in 1993.

Dr. Manoj Gulhane also holds the post of Associate Professor at the NDMVP Medical College in Nashik. He is a member of several associations connected with neurology and related areas, and serves as Secretary of the Maharashtra Association of Neurology.
